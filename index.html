Some common questions
we are often asked

What is a smart contract?

A smart contract is a computer program or a transaction protocol which is intended to automatically execute, control or document legally relevant events and actions according to the terms of a contract or an agreement.

Do I get my money back?

Yes, once you deside to end your subscription or decide to remove your money from a charity fund, all of the money from your initial deposit will be returned to your wallet.

What happens after I deposit money?

Once you select your prefered subscription and deposit the required amount of money, you will immediatly get log in information sent to your email address whcih will allow you to get into the account.

How long do I have to lock up my money for?

Till never! There is no lock up period, you may cancel and get your initial depoist whenever you'd like.

What happens when I cancel a subscription?

Once you deside to cancel a subscription, your reserved money will be returned to your account wallet and will be at your disposal to either withdrawl or use again on another subscription. When you cancel a subscription you will lose access to the subscription once your money is back in your account.

What is the purpose behind LsLs Protocol?

A way for people to use the future yield of their money in order to purchase goods and services right now.
